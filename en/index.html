Projects

Relevant projects.

Projects focused on applied data science, automation, validation, and visualization of agricultural and operational information. The descriptions below summarize applications developed in a corporate environment, without exposing code, sensitive data, or internal information.

Project 01

LIBS Report System

Application

Application to transform raw LIBS soil analysis spreadsheets into standardized internal reports, with preprocessing, filling, interpolation, and Excel export steps.

Technical foundation

Python, Streamlit, Pandas, openpyxl, XlsxWriter, bcrypt authentication, pytest testing, and Excel export.

Expected outcome

Reduction of manual steps in report generation, greater spreadsheet standardization, and traceability between input and output data.

Project 02

Chemical Inventory Control

Application

Operational system for laboratory chemical reagent stock control, including item registration, batches, movements, consumption, solutions, alerts, and data export.

Technical foundation

FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, Pydantic, SQLite/PostgreSQL, Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, MUI, Recharts, and Docker.

Expected outcome

Centralized inventory, movement traceability, and support for shelf life, criticality, and reagent consumption control.

Project 03

Fertilizer Recommendation Calculator 2.0

Application

Web tool to generate liming, gypsum, phosphorus, and potassium recommendations from soil analysis spreadsheets and configurable agronomic parameters.

Technical foundation

Python, FastAPI, Pandas, agronomic rules engine, Next.js, React, MUI, Vercel, Render, and Excel/CSV export.

Expected outcome

Standardization of technical recommendations, generation of per-field demands, and organization of the commercial product and source catalog.

Project 04

GHG Emissions Calculator

Application

Application to quantify greenhouse gas emissions in agricultural and livestock systems, with spreadsheet upload, automatic validation, detailed IPCC methodology calculation, KPI dashboards, and future scenario simulation.

Technical foundation

Python, Streamlit, Pandas, Altair, openpyxl, ReportLab, pytest, Streamlit caching, and CSV, Excel, and PDF export.

Expected outcome

Automation of emissions calculation, visualization of results by client, farm, crop, and season, plus auditable reports for technical analysis.

Projects developed in a corporate context. Repositories, data, and internal details are private.
